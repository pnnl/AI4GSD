The image and data are from BASEGRAIN: https://basement.ethz.ch/download/tools/basegrain.html

The following steps are used to obtain the image and field data
*Download BASEGRAIN2p3_Installer.exe from https://basement.ethz.ch/download/tools/basegrain.html
*Install it
*Open BASEGRAIN2p3
*Change image file to :  C:\Program Files\BASEGRAIN2p3\application\BASEGRAIN2p3georef1775x1989.jpg
*Input scale(mm/px): 0.56
*Click A
*Click: Results analysis
*Click: fig060p14
